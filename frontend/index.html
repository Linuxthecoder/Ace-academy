About Ace Academy

Ace Academy is a premier online school partnered with the Telangana Government, dedicated to providing personalized education from Nursery to Intermediate II year. Our mission is to foster academic excellence and personal growth through a 1:1 teacher-student ratio, ensuring tailored learning experiences.

We follow the British Curriculum alongside SCERT Telangana books, with Telugu as a compulsory subject and options for Urdu or Hindi. Our expert faculty and innovative platform make us the ideal choice for shaping your child's future.
